Planets of the Solar System

A simple overview of the eight planets

Mercury

Smallest, closest to the Sun, very hot days and cold nights.

Venus

Thick acidic atmosphere and extreme greenhouse effect.

Earth

Our home — liquid water, breathable atmosphere, life.

Mars

The Red Planet — cold, dusty, studied for past water and life.

Jupiter

Largest planet, gas giant with a famous Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Gas giant known for its wide, spectacular ring system.

Uranus

Ice giant, tilted axis—appears to roll along its orbit.

Neptune

Farthest known major planet, deep blue with strong winds.

Simple overview — facts are brief summaries.
